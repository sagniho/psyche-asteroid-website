Origins in Greek Mythology

Discover the story of Psyche in Greek mythology and how it inspired the naming of the asteroid.

The myth of Psyche tells the story of a beautiful mortal princess who falls in love with Cupid, the god of love. To prove her love, Psyche is tasked with completing a series of impossible tasks, which she accomplishes with the help of ants, a river god, and other creatures. In the end, Cupid and Psyche are united and Psyche becomes a goddess herself.
