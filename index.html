Psyche Asteroid

The Psyche asteroid is an object in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter. It is about 226 kilometers in diameter and is believed to be the exposed iron core of a protoplanet.

The asteroid was discovered in 1852 by Italian astronomer Annibale de Gasparis. The name "Psyche" comes from the Greek mythological figure of the same name, who was the goddess of the soul.
